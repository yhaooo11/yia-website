We are

A youth leadership group based in West Richmond Community Centre dedicated to helping our community through collaborative meetings, raising awareness, and expanding!


Mission Statement

To work effectively in order to recognize the needs of our communities, assisting in making it a better place through collaborative meetings, volunteering, raising awareness and expanding our group.


Vision Statement

To be a place where like-minded youth can connect and be recognized as an organization that takes action to make a difference.
